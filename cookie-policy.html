Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how the site is used.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies on our website to: (a) ensure the site works correctly; (b) remember your choices (e.g. language); (c) analyze how visitors use the site so we can improve it; (d) support security and fraud prevention. We keep our use minimal and in line with our Privacy Policy.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary: Required for the website to function (e.g. load balancing, security). These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional: Remember your settings and preferences to improve your experience.
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. pages viewed, time spent). We may use first-party or trusted third-party analytics.

4. Your Choices

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect how the website works. For more control over analytics and marketing cookies, you can use your browser’s “Do Not Track” or similar settings where supported.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or the law. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do.
